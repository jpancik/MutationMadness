    Scientist
    Scientist is the main, most important "building" in this game. Your goal is to keep yours alive and at the same time, try to kill the enemy's one. Scientist gives you the ability to purchase temporary and permanent upgrades.



    Frankie Factory
    Cost: 40
    Frankie Factory is the most basic unit spawner, it spawns Frankies. Also, it increases your income by 20 per minute.

    Mutation Lab
    Cost: 80
    Mutation Lab is an advanced unit spawner. It gives you the ability to combine various heads, torsos and legs and create total of 9 different units. Different body parts are responsible for changing stats of your unit and thus making it stronger or weaker compared to other units. Heads affect unit's hit points, torsos it's attack power and legs it's movement speed. Mutation Lab increases your income by 40 per minute.

    Tesla Coil
    Cost: 35
    Tesla Coil is an "automatic offensive and deffensive device". Without using fancy words, it's a tower, which automatically shoots electric shocks at random enemy unit in it's range.

    Tofu Tower
    Cost: 100 , 30
    Tofu Tower, building responsible for most of your income. Without , you wouldn't be able to do almost anything. You will need this one, trust me. It spawns 10 every 10 seconds. To collect , you have to click on it before it disappears.
